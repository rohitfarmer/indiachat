Indian Chatrooms on Decentralized Networks

Welcome!

This website serves as a list of public multi user chatrooms/channels (MUC) on decentralized networks such as XMPP and IRC. In addition to listing popular MUC we also aim to provide tutorials and recommendations on chat clients. It's a community lead project if you want to get involved or list your MUC then please email at swatantra at disroot dot org. We hope you find this website useful - comments are always welcomed.
