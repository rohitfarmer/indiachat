Indian Chatrooms on Decentralized Networks

Welcome!

This website lists public multi-user chatrooms/channels (MUC) on decentralized networks such as XMPP, IRC, and [matrix]. In addition to listing popular MUC, we also aim to provide tutorials and recommendations for chat clients. It's a community lead project. If you want to get involved or list your MUC, please email at swatantra at disroot dot org. We hope you find this website helpful - comments are always welcomed.

XMPP

Chatrooms on XMPP are not specific to a network. You can join a public MUC hosted on any network with a valid Jabber ID (JID). The list below is of the chat rooms across multiple networks. Expand for chat room description and link.

For an updated list of public MUCs search on https://search.jabber.network/
